# How AI Signals Work (Plain English)

The "Campaign Funnel Intelligence" section is like the **"Dashboard Warning Lights"** for your business. It watches your inventory, sales, and social media to tell you exactly what needs your attention right now.

## 🏢 The Three Departments

### 1. The Lookout (The Signal Service)
This is the scout that watches the "radar." 
- **What it does**: It checks the database for any alerts from the AI. If there's no live data, it knows how to show "Example Signals" so the dashboard never looks empty.
- **Why it matters**: It’s the source of truth for what is happening in the business funnel.

### 2. The Command Center (The Logic Hook)
This is the person sitting in front of the monitors making decisions.
- **What it does**: It decides which signals to show, keeps track of when you've "resolved" (fixed) an issue, and manages the loading animations.
- **Why it matters**: It ensures the dashboard responds immediately when you take action on a signal.

### 3. The Display Screen (The Interface)
This is the actual screen you look at.
- **What it does**: It draws the cards, picks the right colors (Primary for Critical, Emerald for Warning, etc.), and shows the correct icons for Inventory or Social Media.
- **Why it matters**: It makes complex business data easy to read and act upon at a glance.

---

## 🛡️ The "Safety Promise"

Just like the Chat feature, the Signals are now in their own "Blast Shield" container. 

We can add new types of signals (like "Shipping" or "Customer Support" signals) by simply updating the **Lookout** department, without any risk of breaking how the **Display Screen** or the **Command Center** works.
